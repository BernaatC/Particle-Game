Guidelines for save files:


1 - General layout:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF_8"?>
<save>
	<particle pos="100,500" charge="1" />
	<particle pos="300,500" charge="-1" />
	...
</save>

particle: Tag that holds all necessary data for defining a static particle.
Attributes:
	- pos: Defines the position of that static particle. Must be represented as two real numbers separated by a comma (without spaces).
	- charge: Defines the charge of the static particle. Must be one of the following values: [-1, 1].
